How to Prepare for Class 12 Biology Board Exam?

  1. Master Diagrams: Practice all important diagrams daily - they carry significant marks
  2. Understand Genetics: Focus on inheritance patterns, DNA structure, and protein synthesis
  3. Learn Biotechnology: Understand techniques like PCR, gene cloning, and their applications
  4. Memorize Diseases: Know causes, symptoms, and prevention of important diseases
  5. Practice Long Answers: Write detailed answers for 5-mark and 8-mark questions
  6. Revise Regularly: Biology requires consistent revision of concepts and terminology
