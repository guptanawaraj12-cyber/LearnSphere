How to Excel in Class 11 Mathematics?

  1. Understand Concepts: Don't just memorize formulas, understand why they work
  2. Practice Daily: Solve at least 5-10 problems every day
  3. Learn Formulas: Create a formula sheet and revise regularly
  4. Solve Previous Papers: Practice with old question papers
  5. Clear Doubts: Don't let doubts accumulate, clear them immediately
