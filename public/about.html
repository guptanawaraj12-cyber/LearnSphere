Who We Are

Gyanu Note is a free educational platform dedicated to providing high-quality study materials for students from Class 11 to Master's level. We believe that quality education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial background.

Founded in 2024, we have been helping thousands of students achieve their academic goals by providing comprehensive notes, study materials, and resources across various subjects and disciplines.

Our Mission

To make quality education accessible to every student by providing free, comprehensive, and easy-to-understand study materials that help them excel in their academic journey.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and comprehensive educational resource platform in Nepal, empowering millions of students to achieve their dreams through quality education.
