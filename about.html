About Us

Our Story

Gyanu Notes was founded with a simple mission: to make quality education accessible to everyone. We believe that every student deserves access to comprehensive study materials that can help them excel in their academic journey.

What started as a small collection of notes has grown into a comprehensive educational platform serving thousands of students across Nepal. Our commitment to quality and accessibility remains at the heart of everything we do.

We continuously work to improve our platform, adding new features and content to better serve our community of learners.
